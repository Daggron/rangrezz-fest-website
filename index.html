Rangrezz

LIGHTS. DRAMA. RANGREZZ
Theatre is the temple and the performers are the preachers. It is life enhancing and the most important part of our lives. It is greatly said by William Shakespeare : "The world is your stage and we are the little parts of the play called life. " Theatre liberates our souls, it is one of the most strongest forms of art, where the magic is created not by the props or the costumes but how effective the people are to deliver their emotions. Bringing this magic onto the stage, the most awaited National theatre fest- Rangrezz awaits the young and budding artists from nationwide. The stage is the temple and dramatists, its preachers. If your voices are the life of nukkad or love for proscenium, the stage, runs in your veins, we are  waiting for you at Chitkara University, Punjab. Get your team ready, practice your roles as the most extravagant rewards await you!!! If you've got the zeal, the passion and the drama,  then register your team and be a part of RANGREZZ'18 on the 5th and 6th of October. Stay tuned for further details.
